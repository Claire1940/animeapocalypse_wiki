By focusing on "Initial State" resets, you can generate a mountain of supplies without ever leaving a secure location.

### Essential Scavenging List
1.  **Empty Noodle Cups:** Provides braised beef, seasoning packets, and fresh noodles.
2.  **Dull Blades:** A rusted fruit knife can be refreshed into a razor-sharp combat tool.
3.  **Empty First Aid Kits:** Restores bandages, antibiotics, and surgical tools.
4.  **Gas Canisters:** Even empty canisters can be reset to provide fuel for cooking and warmth.

Following these steps ensures you have the stamina and resources to reach high-security zones like Bluebay Residences. Remember that the smell of hot food is a beacon for both zombies and desperate survivors; always barricade your location before performing mass refreshes.

## FAQ

**Q: Can the Anime Apocalypse Time Stop gadget be used on other players?**

A: Yes, but it requires significant evolution of the skill. Initially, it only works on non-living objects. Once the user reaches a higher rank, they can "refresh" a person's body to restore health or even remove the zombie virus by resetting the body to its pre-infected state.

**Q: What is the maximum range of the Refresh ability?**

A: In the early stages, the range is strictly a 1-meter radius. However, as the player's power grows, the range can extend to hundreds of yards, allowing for the manipulation of entire buildings and skyscrapers.

**Q: Does refreshing an item consume resources?**

A: No. The **Anime Apocalypse Time Stop gadget** only requires a cooldown period (usually 10 seconds) and the user's mental stamina. It does not require mana or physical materials, making it the most efficient resource generator in the game.

**Q: How does the "Refresh" handle items with multiple parts?**

A: If you target a container like a noodle cup or a first aid kit, the ability resets the entire unit, including all internal components (noodles, seasoning, bandages, etc.) to their factory-sealed condition.
