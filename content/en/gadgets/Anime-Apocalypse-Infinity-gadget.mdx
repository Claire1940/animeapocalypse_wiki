## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Anime Apocalypse Infinity gadget at the start of the game?**

A: The gadget is found on the very last page of the initial prop shop. It is labeled as "Uninvestigated Wealth" and appears as a small gold coin. You must select it within the first 7 seconds of the game's start.

**Q: Can the Infinite Wealth gadget be stolen by other players?**

A: Once selected and pocketed, the ability is bound to your account. However, the physical assets you buy with it (like Future Island) can be attacked, so you must invest in automated defenses and jammers.

**Q: Is the Anime Apocalypse Infinity gadget effective against the Zombie King?**

A: Not directly. The gadget provides money, which you must then use to hire scientists or purchase high-tech drones and antidotes. It is an indirect but highly effective counter to high-level biological threats.

**Q: What happens if I run out of things to buy with the gadget?**

A: In the 2026 version of the game, the wealth is truly infinite. The challenge isn't the amount of money, but the time it takes to convert that money into useful survival assets before the world's social systems collapse.
