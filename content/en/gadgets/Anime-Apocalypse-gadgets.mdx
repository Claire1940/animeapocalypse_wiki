## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the rarest Anime Apocalypse gadgets?**

A: The rarest Mythic and Legendary gadgets are primarily found in the "Nightmare Raid" boss drops or by using high-tier Gacha tickets. Occasionally, developers release limited-time event gadgets that can only be obtained during specific weeks of the year.

**Q: Can I trade gadgets with other players?**

A: Currently, the game does not support a direct trading system for gadgets to prevent market inflation. However, you can gift "Gadget Boxes" to friends through the in-game shop.

**Q: Do gadgets work in PvP modes?**

A: Yes, **Anime Apocalypse gadgets** are active in PvP, but their effects are often normalized to ensure competitive balance. For example, a 25% damage boost in PvE might be reduced to 5% in a PvP arena setting.

**Q: What is the best way to get Gadget Scraps quickly?**

A: The most efficient way to farm scraps is by completing the "Daily Trial" missions and dismantling any low-tier gear you receive from wave completions. Participating in World Boss events also yields a high volume of upgrade materials.
