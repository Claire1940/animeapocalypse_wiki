## Understanding the Trait System in 2026

Traits are passive modifiers assigned to your units upon summoning. While every unit comes with a base set of stats, a trait can add significant multipliers to damage, attack speed, range, or even unique utility effects like cooldown reduction. In the 2026 update, the developers have streamlined the process, moving away from gem-based rerolling to a more specialized token-based system to encourage active gameplay.

When you perform **Anime Apocalypse trait rerolls**, you are essentially gambling for a better modifier. Each roll replaces the existing trait with a new one randomly selected from the pool. It is vital to remember that unless you have a "Trait Lock" item, the previous trait is lost forever once the new one is applied.

## How to Perform Anime Apocalypse Trait Rerolls

To start the reroll process, you must navigate to the Trait Scientist NPC located in the central hub of the Apocalypse City. Interacting with this NPC opens the reroll interface. Here, you can select the unit you wish to modify.

1.  **Select Your Unit:** Drag the unit from your inventory into the primary slot.
2.  **Check Your Currency:** Ensure you have enough Trait Shards or Reroll Tokens.
3.  **Initiate the Reroll:** Click the "Roll" button to generate a new trait.
4.  **Decide to Keep or Discard:** You will be shown the new trait's stats. You must manually choose to "Swap" or "Keep Current."

## Best Traits to Target for Endgame

Not all traits are created equal. Depending on the unit's role—whether they are a DPS (Damage Per Second) dealer, a Buffer, or a Crowd Control (CC) unit—you will want to aim for specific modifiers during your **Anime Apocalypse trait rerolls** sessions.

### DPS Units (Attacker Role)
For your primary damage dealers, you want traits that maximize output. "Godspeed" and "Slayer" remain the gold standards in 2026.
*   **Godspeed (Legendary):** Increases attack speed by 25%. This is often better than raw damage for units with "on-hit" effects.
*   **Reaper (Mythic):** Grants a 5% chance to execute non-boss enemies instantly and increases damage by 80%.

### Support and Utility Units
Support units don't need damage. Instead, look for traits that allow them to cast their abilities more frequently or cover more ground.
*   **Tactician (Epic):** Reduces ability cooldowns by 15%.
*   **Eagle Eye (Rare):** Increases attack range by 20%, ensuring your buffers can reach the front lines from a safe distance.

> **💡 Tip:** If you pull a "Tactician" trait on a pure DPS unit, it is usually worth rerolling. However, on a healer or buffer, it is an S-tier pull.

## Strategies for Efficient Rerolling

Since resources are limited, you should never approach **Anime Apocalypse trait rerolls** without a plan. Blindly clicking the roll button is the fastest way to deplete your inventory with nothing to show for it.

### The "Pity" Mechanic
While not explicitly stated in the UI, the 2026 update includes a hidden pity counter for **Anime Apocalypse trait rerolls**. After 50 rolls on a single unit without seeing a Legendary or higher, your odds for the next roll increase by 0.5% cumulatively. To maximize this:
*   Focus all your tokens on one unit at a time.
*   Do not spread your rolls across multiple characters, as the pity counter is unit-specific.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Many players waste thousands of tokens because they don't understand the math behind the rolls. One of the biggest mistakes is "Over-rolling." If you have a Legendary trait like "Executioner," and you are hunting for a Mythic, the odds are heavily against you (0.4%). Unless you have an abundance of tokens, it is often better to keep the Legendary and move on to your next unit.

Another common error involves the "Infinite Token Cap." In the current 2026 version, there is a weekly cap on how many tokens you can earn from Infinite Mode. If you hit this cap, stop farming and wait for the weekly reset, or focus on Raid content instead.

| Mistake | Consequence | How to Fix |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Replacing Legendary with Rare** | Massive DPS loss | Use Trait Locks or read carefully |
| **Ignoring Range Traits** | Units miss targets | Keep Range traits on short-range units |
| **Rolling without Pity** | Wasted tokens | Focus on one unit until a high tier hits |
| **Spending Gems on Rolls** | Poor value | Use Gems for Summons; use Tokens for Traits |

## FAQ

**Q: Can I trade Trait Tokens with other players in Anime Apocalypse?**

A: No, Trait Tokens and Shards are account-bound items. You must earn them through gameplay, raids, or the premium shop. This ensures that **Anime Apocalypse trait rerolls** remain a mark of a player's progression.

**Q: Is there a way to guarantee a Mythic trait?**

A: There is no 100% guarantee for a Mythic trait. However, using a Golden Token significantly improves your odds by removing all Common and Rare traits from the pool for that specific roll.

**Q: Does the unit's level affect the quality of the trait reroll?**

A: No, the level of your unit does not influence the outcome of **Anime Apocalypse trait rerolls**. A level 1 unit has the same mathematical probability of rolling an Apocalyptic trait as a level 100 unit.

**Q: What should I do if I run out of Trait Shards?**

A: The best way to recover is to clear the Daily Trial missions. These are short, 5-minute challenges that provide a steady stream of Shards. Additionally, participating in any active community events usually yields a high volume of reroll materials.
