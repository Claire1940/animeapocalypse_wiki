## Necrotic Value and Skill Evolution

Eliminating enemies in the 2026 meta grants **Necrotic Value** (also known as Undead Points). Skill evolution is divided into two categories: standard level-ups and **Extraordinary Evolution**.

### Standard Skill Upgrades

## The Death Domain and Undying Invasion

Upon reaching level 30 and completing the first class promotion, players can unlock the **Death Domain**. This is a private sub-dimension that serves as a storage facility for your undead army. The domain's capacity is tied to its level, which is upgraded using **Death Power**.

### Domain Abilities
*   **Undying Army**: Allows for the storage of up to 100,000 units (at Level 4). Stored units do not expire and maintain their duration timers until summoned.
*   **Gate of Death**: A strategic skill that opens a portal to the Death Domain, allowing your entire army to invade a specific target area.
*   **Undying Loyalty**: A passive chance to recruit defeated dark-type bosses into your permanent service.

### Strategic Deployment
In 2026, the most effective way to clear Abyss-difficulty dungeons like Hope City is to prep your army inside the domain first. By storing 100 **Dark Sword Saints** and a swarm of **Shadow Warwolves**, you can initiate an "Invasion" that overwhelms the dungeon's internal mechanics, effectively bypassing the standard "defense" phase of the mission.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the zero-cooldown effect mentioned in this Anime Apocalypse cooldown guide?**

A: The zero-cooldown effect is tied exclusively to the **Necrotic Scourge** EX-rank talent. You must obtain this specific god-tier card to activate the "Infinite Firepower" passive, which removes cooldowns from all necromancy-class skills.

**Q: Does the "Infinite Firepower" talent work on equipment skills?**

A: No. The talent only applies to active class skills (summons and spells). Skills attached to gear, such as the "Shadow Step" on the Shadow Cloak, still follow their native cooldown timers.

**Q: What happens if I run out of mana while using spamming skills?**

A: If your mana hits zero, you can no longer cast spells regardless of the cooldown status. It is highly recommended to carry a **Mana Bottle** (capable of storing 100,000 points) to instantly refill your pool during intense battles.

**Q: Can I use the Death Domain in every dungeon?**

A: The Death Domain can be summoned in most open-world areas and standard dungeons. However, certain "trial" spaces or restricted instances may block the "Gate of Death" skill to maintain specific challenge parameters.
