## Advanced Farming Tips for 2026

To truly master the grind, you must look beyond just which map to play. Efficiency is found in the small details of your gameplay loop. For instance, knowing when to "reset" an Infinite run is crucial. As waves progress, the health of enemies increases exponentially. At a certain point, the time it takes to clear a wave outweighs the rewards. Most experts suggest resetting after Wave 50 or 60 to maintain the highest "items per minute" ratio.

Furthermore, pay close attention to character traits. A Sasuke with a "Damage" or "Cooldown Reduction" trait will clear Shibuya significantly faster than one with a "Health" trait. Using your farmed Trait Rerolls to optimize your farming units is a long-term investment that pays off within hours of gameplay.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the Anime Apocalypse best farming methods for beginners?**

A: Beginners should focus on completing the main story quests to unlock the Shibuya map. Once unlocked, join public Infinite matches. Even if you cannot reach high waves, the base rewards for the first 20 waves are significantly better than early-story missions.

**Q: Is Sasuke really better than Legendary characters for farming?**

A: In the current 2026 meta, Sasuke’s AOE capabilities make him superior for clearing large groups of weak enemies quickly. While some Legendaries deal more single-target damage to bosses, Sasuke’s efficiency in Infinite mode is unmatched for resource gathering.

**Q: How often should I use the conversion station?**

A: You should use the conversion station whenever you have a surplus of materials that aren't required for your current upgrade goals. It is better to have the specific items you need now rather than a stockpile of items you might use months later.

**Q: Can I farm Cursed Fingers solo?**

A: Yes, Cursed Fingers drop in Infinite mode regardless of team size. However, playing in a team usually allows you to reach higher waves where the drop rates for rare items like fingers and keys slightly increase.
