## The Akaza Raid: Mechanics and Strategy

The Akaza Raid is the centerpiece of the **Anime Apocalypse release update**, requiring 5 Raid Tickets to enter. This encounter is not just a test of strength but a test of your resource management.

To succeed in the Akaza Raid, players should prioritize the **Black Hole** ability. This skill is currently considered "overpowered" because it can effectively "stun-lock" the boss. By spamming Black Hole, you can keep Akaza trapped in a single location, allowing you to cycle through your high-damage skills without taking retaliation.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the Black Hole ability as soon as it's off cooldown. It fills up based on damage dealt, so high-DPS weapons like the Coyote will allow you to use it more frequently.

### Maximizing Your Drop Chance
One of the most important additions in this update is the Akaza-themed cosmetic. Unlike other cosmetics that focus purely on damage (like the Akatsuki Robe or Adaptation Wheel), this new item is the only one in the game that provides a **Drop Chance Bonus**. 

If you are farming for the 1% drop rate of the Destructive Fists, equipping this cosmetic is mandatory. While you might lose a small percentage of raw damage, the increased likelihood of rare drops makes it the superior choice for long-term grinding.

## The Grave Walker Difficulty: End-Game Farming

For players looking to farm Runes, XP, and Money, the new **Grave Walker** difficulty is the ultimate challenge. This mode features zombies with significantly higher health pools and movement speeds.

### Survival Requirements
You cannot survive Grave Walker without **Life Steal**. The enemies in this mode deal enough damage to kill a player in seconds. Investing in traits that provide health regeneration on hit is the only way to sustain through the later waves.

### Raid Ticket Farming Efficiency
A common mistake players make is farming for Raid Tickets in the "Infinite" game mode. Based on testing in the **Anime Apocalypse release update**, Infinite mode is inefficient for ticket yields.

| Game Mode | Time Spent | Ticket Yield (Avg) | Efficiency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Infinite Mode** | 60 Minutes | 20 Tickets | Low |
| **Grave Walker** | 15 Minutes | 15-20 Tickets | **High** |
| **Normal Dungeons** | 5 Minutes | 3-8 Tickets | Medium |

For the fastest results, focus on Grave Walker or high-level Normal Dungeons. A 4-minute run in a standard dungeon can often yield more tickets per hour than a grueling hour-long session in Infinite mode.

## Meta Breakdown: Why Coyote Still Rules

Despite the new additions, the **Coyote weapon** remains the strongest DPS option in the game. Its second move, which summons wolves, can deal upwards of 5,000 damage in a single hit when properly optimized with traits like **Reaper**.

### The Ultimate Raid Loadout
If you want to clear the Akaza Raid in under 60 seconds, follow this loadout strategy:
1.  **Primary Weapon:** Coyote (Max Cooldown traits).
2.  **Support Weapon:** Goku (Saying move for the DPS buff).
3.  **Title:** Upper Moon Title (for the extra drop chance) or World 5 Title (for raw power).
4.  **Strategy:** Activate the "Super Saying" buff, unleash the Coyote wolves, and then enter the fight. Use Black Hole to keep the boss stationary and spam the Coyote's second skill.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The "100 Mastery" bug has been patched. You can no longer use high-mastery moves at level 1. You must now grind mastery levels properly to unlock a weapon's full potential.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Akaza weapon in the Anime Apocalypse release update?**

A: There are two ways to obtain the Akaza weapon (Destructive Fists). You can either buy it from the Raid Shop using Raid Coins earned from completing the Akaza Raid, or you can get it as a rare 1% drop from the boss itself.

**Q: What is the best way to farm Raid Tickets?**

A: Avoid Infinite mode if you only want tickets. The most efficient method is running Grave Walker difficulty or fast Normal Dungeons. You can typically earn enough tickets for a raid in about 15 minutes of focused dungeon farming.

**Q: Why is the Black Hole ability so important?**

A: Black Hole provides essential crowd control. In raids, it keeps the boss from moving or attacking, allowing you to deal uninterrupted damage. In Grave Walker mode, it clears entire rooms of fast-moving zombies instantly.

**Q: Which title should I use for farming?**

A: If you are looking for rare drops, the **Upper Moon Title** is currently the best in the game due to its drop chance bonus. If you are struggling to clear content, use the **World 5 Title** for the extra damage boost.
