To execute an effective **anime apocalypse farming route**, you must prioritize mobility and the identification of high-value resource caches. Focus on looting civilian warehouses and agricultural hubs. These locations are often overlooked by aggressive players who prioritize military depots.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most efficient way to gain points on an anime apocalypse farming route?**

A: Focus on high-level mutant hunting using a 10x point multiplier buff. Combining this with environmental traps allows for high point yields while keeping your personal risk low.
