## New Weapons: Akaza and Escanor Showcases

Two of the most anticipated additions to the arsenal are the Akaza and Escanor weapons. These tools aren't just for show; they offer unique utility that can define your playstyle in both raids and standard dungeon clearing.

### The Akaza Weapon (Destructive Fists)
The Akaza weapon is a high-speed, high-damage option primarily obtained through the Akaza Raid. There are two primary ways to secure this weapon:
1. **Direct Drop:** A rare 1% drop chance from defeating the Akaza boss.
2. **Raid Shop:** A guaranteed purchase using Raid Coins if luck isn't on your side.

The weapon features the **Explosive Fury** barrage, which deals massive damage in a short window, and a wide-reaching **Shockwave** for crowd control.

### The Escanor Weapon (Divine Axe Rhitta)
The Escanor weapon offers a different rhythm, focusing on heavy hits and area-of-effect (AoE) clearing. Key moves include **Superior Slash** and the highly mobile **Sun Glide**, which allows players to reposition while dealing damage. While it lacks some of the damage-over-time (DoT) effects some players hoped for, its raw burst damage makes it a solid contender for elite content.

> **💡 Tip:** When using these weapons, look for the **Reaper** trait. It provides a significant boost to damage output that is essential for clearing Grave Walker difficulty efficiently.

## Farming Guide: Grave Walker Difficulty

If you are looking to farm materials, XP, and Raid Tickets, the **Grave Walker** difficulty is your primary destination. However, this mode is not for the faint of heart. Enemies move faster and hit significantly harder than in lower-tier dungeons.

To survive Grave Walker, you should prioritize **Life Steal** stats. Without a way to recover HP mid-fight, the aggressive zombie AI will quickly overwhelm you. Additionally, this mode is the most efficient way to farm Raid Tickets. While Infinite mode provides resources, Grave Walker yields tickets at a much higher rate per minute, making it the preferred choice for those looking to spam raids.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt Grave Walker without a reliable AoE weapon. Single-target weapons will leave you vulnerable to being swarmed.
