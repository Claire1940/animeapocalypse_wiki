## Farming Strategies: Grave Walker vs. Infinite

One of the most discussed topics in the **Anime Apocalypse discord server** is the efficiency of the new Grave Walker difficulty. This mode is designed for players who need to farm runes, XP, and money rapidly. Unlike the Infinite mode, which is better suited for long-term material gathering, Grave Walker provides a concentrated burst of rewards.

*   **Grave Walker Caution:** The enemies in this mode move significantly faster and hit harder. You must bring a weapon with "Life Steal" to survive the onslaught.
*   **The Black Hole Meta:** The Black Hole skill is currently considered "overpowered" (OP) because it allows you to group enemies and stun-lock bosses. In Grave Walker, your meter fills up so quickly that you can essentially spam this move every other room.
*   **Raid Ticket Grinding:** If you are hunting for raid tickets to fight Akaza, avoid Infinite mode. Testing shows that a 15-minute Grave Walker run can net as many tickets as an hour-long Infinite session.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt Grave Walker without a high AoE weapon like Coyote or a reliable stun skill. The "Zombies" can overwhelm your health bar in seconds if you are caught without a cooldown.
