## New Weapons: Akaza and Escanor Showcases

The centerpiece of this update is the arrival of two iconic weapon sets: the Akaza Destructive Fists and the Escanor Divine Axe. These weapons offer distinct playstyles, ranging from high-speed martial arts barrages to devastating area-of-effect (AoE) solar strikes.

### Akaza (Destructive Fists)
The Akaza weapon is a Mythic-tier option primarily obtained through the new raid system. It focuses on rapid-fire strikes and shockwaves that can keep enemies staggered.

*   **Explosive Fury:** A high-speed barrage that deals significant single-target damage, often considered superior to the standard Saiyan barrage.
*   **Shockwave:** A massive AoE move that clears surrounding enemies, making it ideal for the new Grave Walker difficulty.
*   **Ultimate (Destructive Death):** A cinematic, high-damage finisher that is remarkably efficient for clearing boss phases quickly.

### Escanor (Pride)
Escanor’s weapon brings the power of the sun to the battlefield. While it lacks some of the damage-over-time (DoT) effects found in other top-tier weapons, its raw burst damage is formidable.

*   **Superior Slash:** A frontal flame attack that cuts through groups of enemies.
*   **Sun Glide:** A unique mobility move that allows the player to fly across the arena, which is both functional for dodging and highly entertaining to use.
*   **Ultimate (The One):** A long-duration cutscene attack that deals massive damage to everything caught in its path.

## Mastering the Akaza Raid

The Akaza Raid is the ultimate test for players in the latest **Anime Apocalypse update**. To enter, you must spend five Raid Tickets, which have become a highly sought-after resource. The raid features a multi-phase boss fight against Akaza, who utilizes high mobility and powerful melee combinations.

### The "Black Hole" Strategy
One of the most effective ways to "bully" the Akaza boss is by utilizing the Black Hole ability. This skill effectively pins the boss in place, preventing him from initiating his more dangerous dash attacks. By cycling Black Hole with high-DPS skills from the Coyote or Saiyan weapons, teams can clear the raid in under 60 seconds.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not rely on your 100 Mastery moves immediately. A recent patch fixed a bug where these were available at Mastery level 1; you must now grind the weapon to its full potential to unlock its strongest abilities.

## Grave Walker: The New Gold Standard for Farming

The **Anime Apocalypse update** introduces the "Grave Walker" difficulty for dungeons. This mode is significantly harder than previous tiers, with zombies that move faster and deal enough damage to kill unprepared players in seconds. However, the rewards make it the best game mode for farming almost everything in the game.

### Why Farm Grave Walker?
*   **Raid Tickets:** This is currently the most efficient way to obtain Raid Tickets. While Infinite mode can be used, Grave Walker provides a higher density of ticket drops per minute spent.
*   **Experience and Gold:** The scaling for XP and currency is at its peak here.
*   **Runes:** High-tier runes drop more frequently, allowing for better weapon customization.

| Resource | Best Farming Location | Efficiency Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Raid Tickets** | **Grave Walker Dungeons** | High |
| **Crafting Materials** | Infinite Mode | High |
| **Weapon XP** | **Grave Walker Dungeons** | Extreme |
| **Raid Coins** | Akaza Raid | Fixed |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt Grave Walker without a reliable source of **Life Steal**. The enemy damage output is so high that you need to recover health constantly while dealing damage to survive the later rooms.

## Optimized Meta Builds for 2026

While the new weapons are flashy, the **Anime Apocalypse update** hasn't completely dethroned the reigning kings of DPS. For players looking to set record clear times, the combination of the Coyote weapon and the Saiyan support weapon remains the gold standard.

### The Coyote Dominance
Coyote is widely regarded as the highest DPS weapon in the game due to its second skill, which can deal upwards of 5,000 damage in a single hit when properly built. When paired with a full Cooldown Reduction build, you can spam this move to shred boss health bars.

### Support Synergy
The Saiyan weapon, specifically at 100 Mastery, provides a massive damage buff that benefits your entire loadout. Switching to Saiyan to trigger the buff before unleashing Coyote’s wolves is the fastest way to clear the Akaza raid.

### Important Titles and Cosmetics
The update also introduced the **Upper Moon Title** and a new **Akaza Cosmetic**. Unlike other cosmetics that focus purely on damage, the Akaza cosmetic is the only one in the game that currently buffs **Drop Chance**. 

*   **Upper Moon Title:** Provides a significant boost to drop rates, making it essential for farming the 1% Akaza weapon drop.
*   **Collector Title:** Obtained through extensive Infinite mode play; a great secondary option for those who haven't lucked into the Upper Moon drop.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get the Akaza weapon in the Anime Apocalypse update?**

A: There are two main ways: you can either get it as a rare 1% drop from successfully completing the Akaza Raid, or you can save up Raid Coins earned from the raid to purchase it directly from the Raid Shop.

**Q: What is the best way to farm Raid Tickets?**

A: The most efficient method is running the new Grave Walker difficulty in normal dungeons. While Infinite mode also drops tickets, the drop rate per hour is much higher in Grave Walker.

**Q: Is the Escanor weapon better than Coyote for DPS?**

A: While Escanor has impressive burst damage and great AoE, Coyote still holds the title for the highest consistent DPS in the game, especially when built with maximum Cooldown Reduction.

**Q: Why should I use the Akaza cosmetic instead of the Akatsuki robe?**

A: While the Akatsuki robe and Adaptation Wheel provide more raw damage, the Akaza cosmetic is currently the only one in the game that increases your Drop Chance. This makes it superior for farming rare items and weapons.
