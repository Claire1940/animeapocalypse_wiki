## Setting Up the Ultimate AFK Macro for Infinite Mode

To maximize your efficiency, using a macro is highly recommended for overnight farming. The goal is to automate Infinite Mode to stack levels and accessories while you are away from your PC. Based on the most successful community configurations, TinyTask remains the preferred tool due to its low resource overhead and ease of use.

Follow these steps to configure your macro effectively:

1.  **Set Resolution:** Ensure your monitor resolution is set to **1920x1080**. Most community macros are recorded at this specific scale to ensure mouse clicks align with UI buttons.
2.  **Adjust Game Speed:** Set the in-game speed to **3x or 4x**. This ensures the macro cycles through waves faster, maximizing your hourly gains.
3.  **Movement Logic:** A common issue in Anime Apocalypse is the "field" mechanic. If your character stands still, mobs may stop attacking or the game might fail to register progress. The macro should include a "walk forward" command at the start of every cycle to clear the field and trigger mob spawns.
4.  **Ability Spam:** Program the macro to cycle through your unit abilities (keys 1-4) consistently.

## Top Units for AFK Farming in 2026

Not every unit is suitable for an automated setup. For a macro to be successful, you need units with high Area of Effect (AoE) damage and reliable cooldowns. Units that require precise manual aiming should be avoided in favor of those that target automatically or have massive hitboxes.

| Unit Name | Type | Why Use Them? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coyote** | AoE Specialist | Massive range and consistent damage output. |
| **Overlord** | AFK King | High durability and excellent auto-targeting logic. |
| **Sand** | Crowd Control | Slows down enemies, giving your macro more time to react. |
| **Sanji** | DPS | His "B Move" is essential for clearing high-health waves. |
| **Pride** | Single Target | **Avoid** for AFK; his targeting is too narrow for wave clearing. |

When selecting your loadout, focus on **Cooldown (CDR)** upgrades. The faster your units can cycle their abilities, the less likely a mob is to leak through your defenses during a macro loop.

## Optimal Gadgets and Map Selection

The choice of map and gadgets can determine if your AFK session lasts two hours or twelve. For Infinite Mode, you want a map with a straightforward pathing system. Maps with complex turns or multiple spawn points can sometimes confuse the auto-targeting of certain units.

The "Black Hole" gadget is currently considered the gold standard for macro users. It pulls mobs into a centralized point, allowing your AoE units like Coyote to deal maximum damage to the entire wave simultaneously.
