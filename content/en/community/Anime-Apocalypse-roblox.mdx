## Setting Up Your AFK Macro in 2026

To truly "farm everything" while you are away from your computer, you will need to utilize a macro recorder. TinyTask remains the most popular choice for **Anime Apocalypse roblox** players due to its low resource usage and simplicity. However, some players also find success with ThermalTask. 

### Technical Requirements
Before recording your macro, you must ensure your technical environment is consistent. If your settings change between recording and playback, the macro will fail.

1.  **Screen Resolution:** Set your monitor to **1920 x 1080**.
2.  **In-Game Speed:** Ensure the game is set to **3x or 4x speed**.
3.  **Graphics Settings:** Lower your graphics to Level 1 to prevent frame drops that could de-sync the macro.

### Recording the Perfect Loop
The most common mistake players make is forgetting to account for the "aggro field" bug. In the current version of the game, a specific field can sometimes prevent mobs from attacking you correctly, which stalls the wave progression. To fix this, your macro must include a "walk forward" command.

*   **Step 1:** Start the recording in TinyTask.
*   **Step 2:** Enter the match and immediately hold the "W" key to walk forward for 2-3 seconds. This clears the field and ensures mobs aggro correctly.
*   **Step 3:** Spam your unit abilities (typically keys 1-4).
*   **Step 4:** Navigate to the upgrade menu and click the cooldown reduction upgrades.
*   **Step 5:** End the recording and set it to "Continuous Playback."

## Optimization: Gadgets and Ability Upgrades

In **Anime Apocalypse roblox**, units alone aren't enough to reach the highest waves of Infinite Mode. You must supplement your team with the correct gadgets. Gadgets provide passive or active utility that can save a run when your units are on cooldown.

### The Black Hole Meta
Currently, the **Black Hole gadget** is considered essential for AFK farming. It pulls enemies into a centralized point, allowing your AoE units like Coyote or Sand to hit every single mob simultaneously. This synergy prevents "leakers"—mobs that slip past your defenses—from ending your run.

### Upgrade Priority
When your macro is clicking through the upgrade menu, it should prioritize stats in a specific order to ensure maximum uptime for your abilities.

| Priority | Upgrade Type | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Cooldown Reduction** | Increases ability frequency for better wave clearing. |
| **2** | **Area of Effect (Range)** | Ensures units hit mobs further down the path. |
| **3** | **Raw Damage** | Necessary for higher waves where mob HP scales. |
| **4** | **Ability Duration** | Keeps active effects on the field longer. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are struggling with survival, check your accessory inventory. Accessories stack in 2026, meaning you can significantly boost your base stats by farming Infinite Mode overnight and equipping your best drops the following morning.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get banned for using a macro in Anime Apocalypse roblox?**

A: Most Roblox games, including Anime Apocalypse, allow the use of basic macro tools like TinyTask for AFK farming. However, you should always avoid "exploits" or "scripts" that modify game files, as these are against the Roblox Terms of Service and can result in a permanent ban.

**Q: Why does my macro stop working after an hour?**

A: This is usually due to a "de-sync." If your computer experiences a brief lag spike, the macro's timing will be off. To prevent this, ensure your graphics are at the lowest setting and that no other heavy applications are running in the background during your session.

**Q: What is the best resolution for macro recording?**

A: The gold standard for **Anime Apocalypse roblox** macros is 1920 x 1080. Using this resolution ensures that the button coordinates in your macro match the UI elements of the game perfectly.

**Q: Which units should I avoid for Infinite Mode?**

A: You should generally avoid single-target units or units with "knockback" abilities that might push enemies out of your AoE zones. Pride is currently considered one of the weakest units for this mode due to poor efficiency relative to cost.
