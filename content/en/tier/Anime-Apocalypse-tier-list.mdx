### How to Optimize Your Unit

## Strategic Team Compositions

Success in the apocalypse isn't just about having one strong unit; it's about how your team works together. A balanced team should include:

*   **The Carry:** A high-DPS unit (like Sasuke) focused on clearing waves.

*   **The Support:** Units that provide slows, stuns, or economy boosts to help you survive later waves.
