### Hunting the Beast King
Beast Kings, such as the Bone Corroding Dragon Lizard, are found in high-radiation zones like City D's chemical plants. To defeat them, your build must include high-level restraint skills (like Vines) and a finishing move that targets the core heart. 

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never attempt to absorb a Beast King crystal without a high "Willpower" stat, or you risk turning into a mindless "Subject Zero" or a blood puppet.

G City represents the peak of technological and psionic advancement. When entering this zone, your **Anime Apocalypse best build** will be tested by the city's strict laws. Psionics are often forced to wear dampening bracelets, which can reduce your power by up to 90%.

To navigate this, your build should include a "Clone Solidification" skill. This crystal is the only way to permanently stabilize your father or any "Patient Zero" allies you may have recruited.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to level up my abilities in Anime Apocalypse?**

A: The fastest way is to farm mutant hordes during a "Beast Tide." Ensure your build has an AoE (Area of Effect) ability like Fire or Poison Vines to clear small mobs quickly while you focus on the commander for a high-purity crystal drop.

**Q: Can I have more than two abilities at once?**

A: Yes, but it requires the "Sovereign Copyist" path. By consuming a Beast King's core and utilizing blood resonance, you can stack up to five different elemental powers, though this is considered the **Anime Apocalypse best build** for late-game players only.

**Q: What should I do if my Ability Stone is stolen?**

A: If your stone is extracted, your power fluctuation will vanish. However, you can restore your powers by finding a "Bone Corroding Dragon Lizard" and devouring its evolving crystal. This restarts your psionic core and can even lead to a more powerful evolution.

**Q: Is it better to join a squad or play solo?**

A: While solo play is possible with a high Spatial Manipulation stat, joining a balanced squad (Offensive, Speed, Plant, and Support) is recommended for Beast King raids. The synergy between a "Lightning Master" and a "Vine Controller" is often the difference between victory and becoming a monster's nourishment.
