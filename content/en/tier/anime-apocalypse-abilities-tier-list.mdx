## How to Read This Tier List Without Misbuilding

A common mistake is copying a ranking without considering your account stage. This anime apocalypse ability tier list works best when you match tier picks to your current resources.

### Your progression stage matters more than raw tier labels
- **Early game (Mastery 0-30):** Burst and easy clears are king.
- **Mid game (Mastery 30-70):** Consistency and cooldown flow become more important.
- **Late game (Mastery 70-100):** Scaling effects, upgraded forms, and survivability mechanics decide run length.

## Tier-by-Tier Breakdown and Practical Use

### S Tier: Coyote, Saiyan, Pride

These are your premium investments in 2026.

- **Coyote** is a top-end damage choice when fully developed. It may not feel dominant immediately, but once mastery and V-form are online, its output is among the strongest for Raid/Survival loops.
- **Saiyan** is the most plug-and-play elite option. It performs well at low and high mastery, and the revive utility gives it value even when your run gets messy.
- **Pride** offers the most stable “no bad mode” profile. It may not hard-counter one mode better than everything else, but it has few weaknesses.

> **💡 Tip:** If you want one ability that feels strong right away, prioritize **Saiyan** first. If you’re building for max late-game payoff, push **Coyote** to full scaling.

### A Tier: Shadow Monarch, Destructive Fists

Strong choices that can feel S-tier in the right context.

- **Shadow Monarch** can be amazing in Infinite, especially when mastery is high. At low investment, it may feel weaker than expected.
- **Destructive Fists** is very consistent and reliable for broad PvE use. It usually performs smoothly in Raid and Survival but can trail top S-tier damage ceilings.

### B Tier: Sosuke, Firefighter

Useful, especially for transitioning accounts.

- **Sosuke** is arguably the best beginner bridge. Great burst and range make early farming easier than many higher-rarity abilities at base mastery.
- **Firefighter** is solid but rarely exceptional. It works when you need a functional DPS option before pulling or building stronger kits.

### C/D Tier: King of Curses, Zoro, Nen Prodigy

These are not long-term priorities.

- **King of Curses** beats lower tiers but lacks standout strengths.
- **Zoro** and **Nen Prodigy** are starter-level tools and should be replaced once better options become available.
