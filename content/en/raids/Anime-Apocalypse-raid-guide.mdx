## Raid Entry Requirements and Tickets

Before you can face the Akaza boss, you must secure the necessary currency for entry. Unlike standard dungeons, raids require a specific consumable known as Raid Tickets. In the current 2026 meta, these are considered a scarce resource, so efficient farming is mandatory.

| Requirement | Detail | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Entry Cost** | 5 Raid Tickets | Required for every single attempt. |
| **Game Mode** | Raid Mode | Accessible via the main dungeon portal. |
| **Recommended Level** | World 5+ | High damage and life steal are necessary. |
| **Key Item** | Black Hole | Essential for boss crowd control (CC). |

To farm these tickets, you should avoid "Infinite Mode" if your primary goal is speed. While Infinite Mode is excellent for general materials, it is notoriously slow for ticket drops. Instead, focus on the **Grave Walker** difficulty in normal dungeons. A standard Grave Walker run can yield between 3 to 8 tickets in roughly 3 to 4 minutes, making it the most efficient method to sustain your raiding habits.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Grave Walker difficulty features enemies with high walk speeds and massive damage. Ensure you have a weapon with Life Steal traits to survive the onslaught.

## Akaza Boss Mechanics and Strategy

The Akaza raid boss is a formidable opponent with high mobility and devastating barrage attacks. However, he has a significant weakness: he is highly susceptible to stun-locking via the **Black Hole** ability.

### The Black Hole Strategy
The most effective way to "bully" the boss is to cycle your Black Hole ability. This skill traps the boss in place, preventing him from initiating his dash or shockwave attacks. 

1.  **Initiate:** Start the fight by using your secondary support weapon (like Goku/Saiyan) to buff your damage.
2.  **Cast Black Hole:** Aim directly at Akaza to lock him in position.
3.  **Burst Damage:** Switch to your primary DPS weapon (Coyote is highly recommended) and unleash your highest-damage skills.
4.  **Recharge:** Because dealing damage refills your meter, hitting the boss while he is trapped often recharges the Black Hole before the first one expires.

### Akaza's Move Set
If you fail to keep the boss stunned, you must be prepared to dodge the following moves:

*   **Explosive Fury:** A rapid-fire barrage that can shred your health if you are caught in the center.
*   **Shockwave:** A large AoE (Area of Effect) move that hits everything in a wide radius.
*   **Dash:** A quick closing move used to bridge the gap between him and the player.

## Best Weapons for the Akaza Raid

While the new Akaza and Escanor weapons are flashy and powerful, they are not necessarily the absolute "best-in-slot" for clearing the raid in record time. For the fastest clears in 2026, players are leaning toward a specific combination of DPS and Support.

| Weapon | Tier | Role | Why It’s Meta |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coyote** | **Meta** | Main DPS | Highest DPS in the game; 2nd skill deals 5,000+ damage. |
| **Goku (Saiyan)** | **Meta** | Support | 100 Mastery "Saiyan" move provides a massive DPS buff. |
| **Akaza (Fists)** | **High** | DPS/Mobility | Excellent barrage damage and fast wind-up times. |
| **Escanor** | **Mid** | DPS | Fun "Sun Glide" mobility but lacks consistent DoT. |

For the **Coyote** weapon, it is highly recommended to roll for **Max Cooldown** traits. This allows you to spam the second move, which is the primary source of the weapon's absurd damage output. When combined with the Saiyan buff, you can frequently clear the Akaza raid in under 50 seconds.

## Rewards, Drop Rates, and the Raid Shop

The main incentive for running the raid is to obtain the **Destructive Fists** (Akaza's weapon) and the exclusive **Upper Moon** title. 

### Drop Table & Raid Coins
Every successful completion rewards you with **Raid Coins**, typically ranging from 50 to 75 coins per run. These coins can be used in the Raid Shop to "pity" the Akaza weapon if luck is not on your side.

*   **Destructive Fists (Weapon):** 1% Base Drop Chance.
*   **Upper Moon Title:** Extremely Rare (Buffs Drop Chance).
*   **Raid Cosmetic:** Buffs Drop Chance (The only cosmetic currently doing so in 2026).
*   **Raid Coins:** 50-75 per run (Guaranteed).

> **💡 Tip:** Equip the Upper Moon title and the Raid Cosmetic specifically for these runs. While they offer less raw damage than the "Adaptation Wheel," the boost to your drop chance is statistically more valuable when hunting for the 1% weapon drop.

## Efficient Farming: Grave Walker Difficulty

To sustain your raiding, you must master the **Grave Walker** dungeon difficulty. This mode is the premier way to farm XP, Money, and most importantly, Raid Tickets. 

The enemies here move significantly faster than in lower difficulties. To succeed, you should prioritize weapons with large AoE and fast wind-up times. The Coyote weapon excels here because its skills trigger almost instantly, preventing zombies from closing the distance while you are in an animation. Always bring the Black Hole skill to Grave Walker; because the enemy density is so high, your meter will refill almost instantly, allowing you to spam the CC throughout the entire dungeon.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to get Raid Tickets in Anime Apocalypse?**

A: The most efficient method is farming normal dungeons on **Grave Walker** difficulty. You can earn 3-8 tickets per 4-minute run. Avoid Infinite Mode for ticket farming, as the drop rate per hour is significantly lower.

**Q: Can I get the Akaza weapon without relying on RNG?**

A: Yes. While the Destructive Fists have a 1% drop chance from the boss, you can also purchase them directly from the Raid Shop using Raid Coins earned from completing the raid. This acts as a "pity" system for players with poor luck.

**Q: Why is the Coyote weapon considered better than the Akaza weapon for this raid?**

A: In the current **Anime Apocalypse raid guide** meta, Coyote provides higher burst damage through its second skill (dealing over 5,000 damage per hit). Its fast wind-up times also make it safer to use against aggressive bosses compared to the longer animations of other Mythic weapons.

**Q: Does the "Upper Moon" title actually help with drops?**

A: Yes, the Upper Moon title provides a substantial boost to your Drop Chance stat. In Anime Apocalypse, maximizing your drop chance is crucial for obtaining rare items like the Destructive Fists and exclusive raid cosmetics.
