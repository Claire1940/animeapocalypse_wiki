## New Weapons Showcase: Akaza and Escanor

The **Anime Apocalypse raid update** introduces two standout weapons that cater to different playstyles. The Akaza weapon (Destructive Fists) focuses on rapid-fire strikes and shockwaves, while the Escanor weapon provides a mix of high-impact slashes and unique mobility.

### Akaza Weapon (Destructive Fists)
This weapon is built for high-intensity melee combat. Its "Explosive Fury" move acts as a superior barrage compared to older weapons like the Saiyan Barrage. It also features a massive AoE shockwave that clears mobs instantly.

### Escanor Weapon
While some players find the Escanor moveset "basic," its raw power is undeniable. The "Superior Slash" deals significant initial damage, though it lacks a Damage Over Time (DoT) component. The standout move is "Sun Glide," which allows the player to fly around the arena, making it excellent for repositioning during chaotic boss fights.

## Meta Strategies for Raid Completion

While the new weapons are flashy, they aren't necessarily the "strongest" for speed-clearing the Akaza raid. To achieve sub-one-minute clears, players are leaning on established meta-favorites combined with new tactical insights.

| Weapon Role | Recommended Weapon | Key Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary DPS** | **Coyote** | Highest DPS in the game; Wolves provide constant pressure. |
| **Support/Buff** | **Goku (Saiyan)** | Mastery 100 provides a massive damage multiplier. |
| **Crowd Control** | **Black Hole** | Essential for locking bosses in place. |
| **Entry Level** | **Sasuke** | High-tier legendary that rivals some mythics for beginners. |

To maximize efficiency, start the fight by activating your "Super Saiyan" buff and unleashing the Coyote wolves before the boss cutscene ends. This allows you to begin dealing damage the millisecond the fight starts. Use the second skill of the Coyote weapon—which can deal upwards of 5,000 damage per hit—and cycle back to the Black Hole to keep the boss stationary.

## Grave Walker: The New Farming Standard

The addition of the Grave Walker difficulty has shifted how players approach resource gathering. This mode is significantly harder than previous iterations, with zombies that move faster and deal enough damage to end a run in seconds. However, the rewards justify the risk.

Grave Walker is currently the most efficient way to farm:
*   **Runes:** Essential for weapon upgrades.
*   **Experience (XP):** Best for hitting the 2026 level caps.
*   **Raid Tickets:** Unlike Infinite mode, which may only yield a few tickets per hour, Grave Walker dungeons can provide 4–8 tickets in a single 4-minute run.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt Grave Walker without a reliable source of Life Steal. The enemy density is so high that you will inevitably take damage; without the ability to recover health on hit, your run will end prematurely.

## Maximizing Your Drop Chance

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get Raid Tickets in the Anime Apocalypse raid update?**

A: The most efficient way to get Raid Tickets is by farming the new Grave Walker difficulty in normal dungeons. While Infinite mode provides some tickets, the drop rate per minute is significantly higher in Grave Walker.

**Q: Is the Akaza weapon better than the Coyote weapon?**

A: In terms of raw DPS and speed-clearing, the Coyote weapon remains the meta-king. However, the Akaza weapon (Destructive Fists) is an excellent alternative with high AoE capabilities and is arguably easier to obtain for players who grind the raid shop.

**Q: Can I use codes to get the new weapons?**

A: Codes in Anime Apocalypse generally provide currency, gems, or tickets rather than direct weapon drops. Always check the "Update" tab in the game menu for the most recent 2026 codes to fund your raid ticket purchases.

**Q: What is the best strategy for the Akaza boss?**

A: The most effective strategy is the "Stun-Lock" method. Use the Black Hole ability to keep Akaza in place while cycling through high-damage skills from the Coyote or Saiyan weapons. Ensure you have high cooldown reduction on your traits to keep the Black Hole available.
