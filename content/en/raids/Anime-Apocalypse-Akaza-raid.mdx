## Step-by-Step Akaza Strategy

Defeating Akaza requires more than just raw power; you must exploit the boss's AI and vulnerability windows. Follow these steps to ensure a smooth run:

1.  **Pre-Buffing:** Before the fight starts, activate your transformations. If you are using Goku, go Super Sayan immediately. If using Coyote, unleash your wolves before the cutscene triggers.
2.  **The Black Hole Loop:** The most effective way to "bully" Akaza is by using the Black Hole skill. This skill sucks the boss into a singularity, preventing him from using his devastating AoE attacks. 
3.  **Rotation:** Start with your highest damage skill (Coyote's 2nd move), immediately follow up with a Black Hole, then cycle through your other skills while the boss is trapped.
4.  **Managing Mastery:** Note that the previous bug allowing 100 Mastery moves at Level 1 has been patched. You must now manually grind your weapons to unlock their full potential before they are viable for high-speed raid clears.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Akaza has high mobility. If you miss your Black Hole placement, he can quickly dash across the arena and one-shot under-geared players. Always aim for the center of his hit-box.

## Farming Raid Tickets: The Grave Walker Method

To maintain a steady supply of tickets for the **Anime Apocalypse Akaza raid**, you must master the Grave Walker difficulty. This mode is significantly harder than standard dungeons, featuring zombies with high movement speed and massive damage output.

To survive Grave Walker, you must equip the **Reaper Trait** or any form of **Life Steal**. Without health regeneration, the chip damage from the fast-moving mobs will quickly overwhelm you. Using the Coyote weapon here is also recommended due to its wide AoE, allowing you to clear entire rooms while staying out of reach of the zombies.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get more raid tickets for the Anime Apocalypse Akaza raid?**

A: The most efficient way to farm tickets is by running normal dungeons on the Grave Walker difficulty. Avoid Infinite mode if your goal is strictly ticket farming, as the drop-per-minute ratio is much lower there.

**Q: Is the Akaza weapon better than Coyote?**

A: In terms of raw single-target DPS for raids, Coyote is still superior due to its high-scaling second skill. However, the Akaza weapon (Destructive Fists) is better for clearing large groups of enemies in dungeons because of its superior AoE.

**Q: Does the drop chance bonus actually work on the 1% weapon drop?**

A: Yes, testing has shown that the drop chance bonus from the Akaza cosmetic and the Upper Moon title significantly impacts the frequency of rare drops. It is highly recommended to prioritize these items if you are farming for mythic weapons.

**Q: What is the best strategy for soloing the raid?**

A: The "Black Hole Loop" is the best strategy. By timing your Black Hole correctly, you can keep Akaza stunned for nearly the entire fight, allowing you to cycle through your high-damage skills without taking any return damage.
