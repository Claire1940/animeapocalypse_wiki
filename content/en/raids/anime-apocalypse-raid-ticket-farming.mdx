## Managing Your Inventory and Resources

Inventory space is a hidden bottleneck in **anime apocalypse raid ticket farming**. If your bags are full of low-tier junk, you will be forced to stop farming to sell or discard items, which ruins your rhythm.

- **Auto-Salvage**: Enable the auto-salvage feature for common and rare gear to keep your inventory clear.
- **Material Stacking**: Prioritize stacking materials in your vault to ensure you have room for incoming drops.
- **Ticket Caps**: Be aware of the maximum ticket limit. If you are near the cap, spend them immediately to avoid wasting incoming daily rewards.
