## Essential Raid Utilities: The Black Hole

Regardless of which weapon you choose as the **Anime Apocalypse best weapon for raids**, your utility slot must be occupied by the **Black Hole**. This skill is arguably the most "broken" mechanic for boss fights in 2026.

When you cast Black Hole, the raid boss becomes stuck in the center of the vortex. This not only deals continuous damage but prevents the boss from performing their own high-damage mechanics. By alternating between your weapon skills and the Black Hole, you can effectively negate the boss's AI. Since the Black Hole meter fills based on damage dealt, using a high-DPS weapon like Coyote ensures that your utility skill is almost always ready to use again.

## Farming Raid Tickets and Resources

To even enter a raid, you need 5 Raid Tickets. In the current 2026 version of the game, these tickets can be difficult to come by if you are farming the wrong modes.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid farming Infinite Mode if your primary goal is Raid Tickets. Testing shows that Infinite Mode yields significantly fewer tickets per hour compared to standard Dungeons.

2.  **Normal Dungeons:** If Grave Walker is too difficult, stick to high-level normal dungeons. You can expect 3 to 8 tickets per successful run.
3.  **Raid Shop:** Use your surplus Raid Coins to buy specific materials, but prioritize the Akaza weapon first if you haven't dropped it yet.
