## How to Obtain the Akaza Weapon

Securing the **Anime Apocalypse Akaza weapon** involves participating in the specialized Akaza Raid. Unlike standard dungeon drops, this weapon is tied to a specific boss encounter that tests your ability to manage cooldowns and dodge high-damage strikes. There are two primary pathways to adding this weapon to your inventory:

### Acquisition Methods

| Method | Requirement | Probability/Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Raid Shop Purchase** | Accumulated Raid Coins | Fixed Cost (Guaranteed) |
| **Boss Drop** | Clear Akaza Raid | **1% Base Drop Chance** |
| **Raid Tickets** | Entrance Fee | 5 Tickets per Run |

The dual-path acquisition system ensures that even if luck is not on your side, you will eventually obtain the weapon through persistent grinding of Raid Coins. Based on current 2026 testing, the 1% drop rate is significantly influenced by your "Drop Chance" stat, making certain titles and cosmetics vital for efficient farming.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt the Akaza Raid without a full stack of Raid Tickets. You can farm these most efficiently in the Grave Walker difficulty dungeons rather than the Infinite mode.

## The Akaza Raid Strategy

To successfully farm the **Anime Apocalypse Akaza weapon**, you must master the Akaza Raid boss. While the boss possesses high health and dangerous AoE attacks, he is susceptible to specific crowd-control (CC) abilities.

### The "Black Hole" Cheese
One of the most effective strategies involves using the **Black Hole** skill. By timing your Black Hole casts, you can effectively "stuck" the boss in place. This prevents the boss from executing his more dangerous combos while you cycle through your high-damage skills like Explosive Fury and Shockwave.

### Recommended Loadout for Raids
While the Akaza weapon is great, you may want to use a specific "meta" build to farm the raid faster:
*   **Primary Weapon:** Coyote (Highest DPS in the game for 2026).
*   **Support Weapon:** Goku/Saiyan (The 100 Mastery "Saiyan" move provides a massive damage buff).
*   **Essential Trait:** Reaper (Look for this to maximize your damage output).

## Maximizing Drop Chances

Since the **Anime Apocalypse Akaza weapon** has a low 1% drop rate, you should prioritize the **Upper Moon Title**. This is currently the only cosmetic/title combination in the game that provides a direct buff to your drop chance percentage. While other cosmetics like the "Adaptation Wheel" offer more raw damage, the efficiency of farming the Destructive Fists relies entirely on how high you can push your drop luck.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find yourself lacking damage while wearing drop-chance gear, unequip the stats from your drop-chance items and "stat-swap" them with damage-focused gear like the Akatsuki Robe or Adaptation Wheel.

## Farming Raid Tickets: Grave Walker vs. Infinite

A common mistake players make in 2026 is farming the Infinite mode for Raid Tickets. While Infinite is excellent for crafting materials, it is notoriously slow for ticket drops. For those hunting the **Anime Apocalypse Akaza weapon**, the **Grave Walker** difficulty is the superior choice.

### Reward Efficiency Comparison

| Feature | Grave Walker Dungeons | Infinite Mode |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Raid Ticket Rate** | High (Up to 8 per 4-minute run) | Low (Approx. 20 per hour) |
| **Difficulty** | High (Requires Life Steal) | Scaling (Starts Easy) |
| **Best For** | Tickets, XP, and Money | Crafting Materials |

When running Grave Walker, ensure you have a weapon with **Life Steal**. The enemies in this mode move significantly faster and hit harder; without a way to recover HP, even high-level players can be overwhelmed in seconds.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Anime Apocalypse Akaza weapon without doing the raid?**

A: No, the Akaza weapon (Destructive Fists) is exclusive to the Akaza Raid boss. You must either get it as a 1% drop from the boss or purchase it from the Raid Shop using coins earned by defeating him.

**Q: What is the best trait to roll on the Destructive Fists?**

A: The **Reaper** trait is currently considered the best for overall DPS. However, if you are struggling with survivability in Grave Walker dungeons, a Life Steal trait can be a viable alternative.

**Q: Does the "Upper Moon" title actually help with the Akaza weapon drop?**

A: Yes. Testing in 2026 confirms that the drop chance bonus from the Upper Moon title significantly affects the 1% drop rate of raid weapons, making it the most valuable title for farmers.

**Q: Is the Akaza weapon better than the Escanor (Pride) weapon?**

A: In terms of speed and efficiency, yes. The Akaza weapon's ultimate is much faster and allows for more frequent rotations, whereas the Escanor weapon has a longer cinematic that can slow down your clear times in high-speed raids.
