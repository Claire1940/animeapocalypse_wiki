## Mastering the Meta: Coyote and Goku

While the new raid weapons are flashy, the community-consensus "Meta" still revolves around the Coyote weapon for pure damage and the Goku weapon for support. If your goal is to clear the Akaza raid in under 60 seconds, these are the tools you need.

### Why Coyote is King
The Coyote weapon remains the highest DPS weapon in Anime Apocalypse. Its strength lies in its second skill, "Wolves," which can deal upwards of 5,000 damage per activation when properly built.

1.  **Trait Synergy:** Aim for the **Reaper** trait on Coyote to maximize execute damage.
2.  **Stat Focus:** Invest heavily in **Cooldown Reduction**. Because Coyote's second move is so powerful, being able to spam it is more beneficial than raw damage stats.
3.  **Strategy:** Pair Coyote with the **Black Hole** ability. Use Black Hole to group enemies or stall a boss, then unleash the Coyote's wolf barrage to shred their health bars.

### The Goku Support Build
The Goku weapon (often referred to as "Saying" in the community) is the premier support tool. Once you reach 100 mastery, the "Saying" transformation provides a significant DPS buff to your other equipped weapons.

*   **The Swap Strategy:** Start a fight by activating the Goku transformation, then immediately swap to Coyote to benefit from the increased damage stats while using Coyote's superior skill set.

## Farming and Progression: The Grave Walker Difficulty

To participate in the **Anime Apocalypse weapon showcase** yourself, you will need to farm resources efficiently. The 2026 update introduces the **Grave Walker** difficulty, which is now the gold standard for farming XP, money, and runes.

### Grave Walker Survival Tips
This difficulty is not for the faint of heart. The zombies are faster and deal significantly more damage than those in lower tiers.

*   **Life Steal is Mandatory:** You cannot rely on natural regeneration. Ensure your weapons have Life Steal traits or runes to recover health while attacking.
*   **AoE Dominance:** Use weapons with large hitboxes to keep the horde at bay.
*   **Black Hole Spam:** Grave Walker features so many enemies that your Black Hole meter will fill almost instantly. You can effectively use Black Hole in every other room to keep the mobs controlled.

### Raid Ticket Efficiency
Raid tickets are the currency required to enter boss fights (costing 5 tickets per entry). While they can drop in Infinite mode, the drop rate is notoriously low.

| Game Mode | Time Investment | Ticket Yield | Efficiency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Infinite Mode** | 60 Minutes | ~20 Tickets | Low |
| **Grave Walker Dungeons** | 15 Minutes | ~20 Tickets | **High** |
| **Normal Dungeons** | 10 Minutes | 3-8 Tickets | Medium |

> **💡 Tip:** If your goal is specifically to farm for the Akaza weapon, stay out of Infinite mode. Stick to Grave Walker or Normal dungeons to maximize your tickets-per-hour.

## Advanced Raid Strategy: The 40-Second Clear

To clear the Akaza raid efficiently, follow this specific rotation:

1.  Equip **Coyote** and **Goku**.
2.  Before the boss spawns, activate the **Goku transformation**.
3.  As the cutscene ends, immediately use **Black Hole** to pin the boss.
4.  Activate Coyote's **Wolf Barrage** (Skill 2).
5.  Cycle between Black Hole and Wolf Barrage. If the boss moves, use the Akaza weapon's **Dash** to stay in range.
6.  Using this method, players can earn up to 73 Raid Coins in under 50 seconds.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the latest codes for Anime Apocalypse?**

A: In the 2026 version, codes are found directly within the game's **Update Tab**. Developers have moved away from external social media codes to ensure all players have access to the same rewards upon release.

**Q: Is the Sasuke weapon worth keeping?**

A: Yes. While it is a Legendary and not a Mythic, Sasuke is considered a "high-tier Legendary" that performs on par with some Mythics like Shadow Monarch. It is an excellent bridge weapon for beginners participating in their first **Anime Apocalypse weapon showcase**.

**Q: What is the best way to fill the Black Hole meter?**

A: The meter fills based on damage dealt. In high-density modes like Grave Walker, the sheer number of enemies allows you to recharge the ability almost instantly. In boss fights, high-DPS weapons like Coyote are required to keep the meter moving.

**Q: Does Drop Chance affect Raid Coin yields?**

A: No, Drop Chance primarily affects the 1% weapon drops and cosmetics. Raid Coin yields are generally fixed between 50 and 75 coins per successful run, depending on your performance and clear speed.
